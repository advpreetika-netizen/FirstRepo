About Pheran Valley

Pheran Valley is dedicated to bringing authentic Kashmiri pherans from the heart of Kashmir to your home. Every piece is thoughtfully chosen, highlighting the craft of local artisans—from intricate embroidery to rich, warm fabrics.

We focus on small, curated batches so that each pheran feels special. When you place an order with us, we personally confirm details like size, fabric, and embroidery before your piece is shipped.
